Venue

As you all know by now the wedding venue is going to be in Sheffield at Mosborough Hall. Please see below for information on the venue including directions and contact details. Rooms at the hotel have been agreed at £110 for the night (usually £140) just mention you'll be staying for the Llewellyn-Armstrong wedding to get the reduced rate.

An old 12th Century manor house, Mosborough Hall was our first choice for our wedding. This is also the venue Lauren's parents held their wedding reception over 32 years ago. The place has been revamped since then and has been home to many nights drinking in the hotel's swanky bar, and the food ain't too bad either.

The wedding will be held in it's own building called the Chatsworth Suite, where the ceremony will be and they'll convert the room for the meal and speeches. In the evening they'll move some tables around for the disco for us to break down on the dancefloor!

Directions: Travelling up t'North

Leave the M1 at junction 30. Turn left at the roundabout and follow the road straight for a couple of miles. You'll come to some traffic lights further on, again just go straight ahead at the lights and you'll pass by The Mossbrook pub on the right, zoom up the hill and at the top the hotel should be on the right.
